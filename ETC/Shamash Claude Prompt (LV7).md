# Shamash — Level 7 Changes

## HP
**New maximum: 64 HP**

---

## New Feature: War Magic
When you cast a cantrip on your turn, you can make one weapon attack as a **bonus action** immediately after.

**The combo:** Cast Frostbite (action) → greatsword swing (bonus action)

---

## New Spell: Aid (Level 2, Abjuration)
Boosts the max and current HP of up to 3 creatures by 5 each. Lasts 8 hours. No attack roll, no save — it just works. Great before a tough fight.

---

## Updated Numbers (proficiency bonus +3 → +4)

| Stat | Old | New |
|---|---|---|
| Proficiency Bonus | +3 | +4 |
| Greatsword/Longsword Attack | +6 | +8 |
| Spell Save DC | 12 | 13 |
| Spell Attack Bonus | +4 | +5 |

**Skills/saves that go up by 1:** Athletics, History, Intimidation, Perception, Strength save, Constitution save

---

## Combat Turn Reference

**Action** — Cast Frostbite (enemy rolls CON save vs DC 13 — if they fail, roll 2d6 cold damage and they get disadvantage on their next attack)

**Bonus Action** — War Magic greatsword swing (d20 +8 to hit / 2d6 +4 damage — reroll any 1s or 2s on damage)

**Next turn** — Extra Attack: two swings (d20 +8 to hit / 2d6 +4 each — reroll 1s and 2s)

**On a crit or kill** — Bonus GWM attack: d20 +3 to hit / 2d6 +14 damage
