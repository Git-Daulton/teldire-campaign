# Session 1 - Of Marrow & Maple

## Session Notes

The party found themselves transported back to Teldire, arriving in front of [[Samwell]]'s old abode. In front of them stood a modest, aged cottage with an attached two-story tower. Taking but a moment to enjoy the familiar air and golden-wheat field scenery, [[Zalros]] made his way to the front door. The door appeared already opened and very slightly ajar, with evidence of force having been used to open it. ^cottage-arrival

Peeking his head in slightly, [[Zalros]] immediately took note of a few shapes in the dark cabin - a chair with a rope potentially indicating a hostage-like situation, and what appeared to be a carpet covered in gems, though it was hard to make out.

[[Elara]] decided to join [[Zalros]] in stepping inside, causing sensor lights to activate and illuminate the space. With the new light, they were able to see shattered glass and two silver vases with red marbles inlaid, tipped on their sides. They also took note of a candlestick with blue marbles engraved into it, a silver water pitcher with a blue marble, and a porcelain doll. ^cottage-interior

Against the far wall were three paintings. On the left, a painting of the coast. On the right, a painting of a black owlbear which looked quite similar to [[Oswald]]. The middle painting, vertical and the largest of the bunch, depicted a young Firbolg assumed to be [[Samwell]]. He was with a Hippogriff in the painting.

[[Brog]] began to approach the doll, but [[Elara]] was focused on testing the painting, remembering that [[Samwell]] had advised the party to enter his basement from a "painting from his youth." [[Brog]] began panicking as he started to hear voices coming from the doll, but [[Elara]] had already begun to enter the painting, so the party moved together as one into the room behind it. Parting with the doll, [[Brog]] heard it say "I only wanted candy..." ^porcelain-doll

> [!quote] Memorable Quote
> "I only wanted candy..." - The porcelain doll, as the party left the cottage

---

Arriving at the bottom of the stairs, the party discovered a small hallway with a door at the end. [[Elara]] went to check the door, discovering it had an arcane lock which had been unsuccessfully picked. The first notch of the arcane lock had failed while being picked, causing a brief fiery burst to destroy the lock. With the lock destroyed, the party continued into the next room. ^basement-entry

In the next room, the party found bookshelves and display cases containing insect specimens and other oddities. [[Zalros]] began looking through a bookshelf and found a book which granted him advantage against aberrations. [[Brog]] located and took some books on creature and monster biology. [[Shamash]] found a book in the bookcase which was hollowed out and contained a small, arcane key. ^specimen-room

> [!tip] Loot Acquired
> **Aberrations book** - Grants [[Zalros]] advantage against aberrations.
> **Biology books** - Creature and monster biology references, taken by [[Brog]].
> **Arcane key** - Found by [[Shamash]] inside a hollowed-out book.

Taking note of the displays, the party noticed a fossil with some missing pieces in the room. [[Brog]] confirmed that there seemed to be a connection between the fossil and the bookcase, as they shared the same framing and border. ^fossil-connection

[[Elara]] decided to go back and check a potential other path in the first hallway after the stairs. Checking closely, she found a sliding door and opened it to reveal a room containing a cabinet with a geode and a terracotta vase, with a chest next to it. On the other wall, there was a cabinet with a bag of birdseed alongside other geodes and rocks. [[Elara]] looked into some of the displays and found one goldfish that looked very unlike the others - it had the head of a dragon and a single silver scale on it. [[Elara]] also found a fossil piece hidden within a structure of a small bird. ^sliding-door-room

Investigating the geodes further, [[Elara]] found an amethyst geode that contained some sort of magical property, worth about 15 gp. [[Elara]] took a pocket full of birdseed and headed back towards the group. [[Drakka]] attempted to prank [[Elara]] by throwing a spider specimen at her, but [[Elara]] responded by saying they were kind of cute, and that her sister caught these all the time growing up. ^amethyst-geode

[[Elara]] told the party of what she had found, and the party returned to the other room to investigate the chest. [[Drakka]] covered [[Brog]] with insect specimens as a prank while [[Brog]] was casting a ritual spell to summon familiars. In the other room, [[Shamash]] used the key he had found to open the chest, and discovered a glowing pearl-like orb. [[Zalros]] cast Identify and discovered it was a Gem of Brightness. ^gem-of-brightness

> [!tip] Loot Acquired
> **Gem of Brightness** - Wondrous Item, Uncommon. A prism with 50 charges that can shed bright light (no charge), fire a blinding beam at a creature within 60 ft (1 charge, DC 15 CON save), or flare in a 30-ft cone (5 charges). Becomes a nonmagical 50 GP jewel when depleted. Held by [[Shamash]].

[[Brog]] completed summoning his two familiars - a bat-like creature and a pseudodragon named Hootie. He asked them to scout ahead, and the party continued down the next hallway with [[Zalros]] in the lead. [[Zalros]] caught an iron-like smell and saw what appeared to be blood on the ground. Investigating further, it appeared to be quite a massacre, with blood continuing down the stairs ahead. [[Brog]] sent his familiars to investigate, and they confirmed there was a body on the floor below. It had been absolutely brutalized and was barely recognizable. ^basement-bodies

The party continued down into the hallway, which had a flickering light. [[Zalros]] took the lead into a room containing an autopsy table made of wood and a pillar with a bowl of black, swirling material inside. The party investigated the corpse further, and [[Elara]] checked it for any materials. Finding some gold, silver, and stolen wizard components, [[Zalros]] removed the blood with Prestidigitation and the party noted the body appeared to be a plains marauder, similar to the ones they had first encountered in the dugout.

[[Zalros]] began casting Identify on the bowl as the party continued on. [[Drakka]] left the party and investigated another room containing a bookcase as well as many knickknacks and specimens. [[Drakka]] found another fossil with missing pieces in this room, and [[Elara]] placed the fossil piece she had found earlier inside. The bookcase shuddered and glowed briefly. ^fossil-placed

The party continued to another room containing a body and a large glass aquarium. [[Elara]] checked the body, finding a silver dagger and some more money. [[Brog]] discovered a large entity inside the aquarium, and [[Shamash]] used the stone to commune with [[Samwell]] and ask about the bodies and the entity. [[Samwell]] replied that the entity was Jerry, and didn't know about the bodies. [[Brog]] confirmed that Jerry was a "seahorse" based on his newly obtained books, and discovered that Jerry may have been up to 3,000 years old. ^jerry-discovered

> [!info] Lore Drop
> **Jerry** - A massive seahorse-like entity housed in [[Samwell]]'s aquarium, potentially up to 3,000 years old.

Investigating the room further, [[Elara]] found a fin piece and placed it in the other fossil, causing the same shudder and glow to occur. [[Zalros]] completed his Identification of the bowl and discovered it was a scrying portal. ^scrying-portal

> [!info] Lore Drop
> **Scrying Portal** - Can target any creature on the same plane of existence (provided you know them or have a physical connection) or a specific location you have seen before. Cannot scry across planes. The sensor moves with creatures but remains fixed on locations.

The party continued further down the hallway, noting a melted sword and further viscera trailing away from them. They discovered a pile of viscera, a beheaded body, and a floor covered in blood. Off to the side, they noted another hallway with a podium containing another bowl similar to the scrying portal. The water was different - more clear yet foggy. Behind that sat a large ant farm with two ants who appeared to stand out from the rest. ^second-scrying-portal

[[Brog]] communicated with the ants and discovered they were two polymorphed humans that had been living as ants for almost 15 years. The party agreed to take them along in return for helping them obtain the fossil pieces that were within the ant colony. Using the newly obtained fossil pieces, the party split up and placed the remaining pieces within the fossil displays they had found earlier. ^ant-people-found

Behind the fish fossil, [[Elara]], [[Zalros]], and [[Brog]] found some bundles of dove maple, as well as fine carpenter's tools that they took along. The dove maple was stamped with the name Alqua - a keeper of woods in the winter court. Behind the rat fossil, [[Drakka]] and [[Shamash]] found a small living biome of moss, vines, fireflies, and mushrooms. ^dove-maple-found

> [!tip] Loot Acquired
> **Dove maple and carpenter's tools** - Fine woodworking supplies stamped with the name Alqua, a keeper of woods in the winter court.

During their exploration, [[Brog]]'s familiars returned to him terrified, noting that something was coming quickly and they had best prepare. The team behind the fish door began to return to find [[Shamash]] and [[Drakka]] fighting a massive suit of living armor, heat radiating from its red essence binding it together. It wielded a massive, jagged blade. ^helmed-horror-fight

The party had an intense fight, with [[Drakka]] and [[Zalros]] taking some nasty hits from the large blade. The animated suit of armor attacked relentlessly, both swinging and attempting to grab party members; it attacked with the finesse of a well-trained soldier. [[Drakka]] dealt a decisive blow to its helmet, caving it in and causing the creation to fade into dust.

Once safe, the party took a moment to consume some healing potions and catch their breath. [[Drakka]] consumed some of the mushrooms they had found, and noted that they got a pleasant feeling that gave them advantage against deception and persuasion from fey. The rest of the party consumed some as well, to varying degrees of effect. [[Brog]] consumed one too many and began to feel a bit queasy, seeing a door to another dimension shimmer in and out of existence briefly. ^fey-mushrooms

> [!tip] Loot Acquired
> **Fey mushrooms** - Grant advantage against deception and persuasion from fey. [[Brog]] ate one too many and briefly glimpsed a door to another dimension.

After giving the ant-people a pinch of birdseed, the party continued to another room which contained a grand arcane lock. This matched the key that [[Zalros]] had from [[Samwell]]; opening the door, the party found two podiums with cushions. One held a small wooden figure painted the colour of charcoal, the other held a figure painted green. ^figurine-room-found

Before the session ended, [[Brog]] heard some clanking and footsteps from a hallway further ahead. The party had begun to think about the encounter - it was likely the broken vases on the first floor which had summoned these creations. With two vases broken, it was safe to assume there was likely another one waiting for the party ahead. ^session-1-cliffhanger

> [!warning] Cliffhanger
> Two new figurines found on podiums - one charcoal, one green. Footsteps and clanking echo from a hallway further ahead. With two broken vases having summoned the previous Helmed Horror, a third may be waiting.

## Next Session Recap

The party had spent days in a strange demiplane - fighting aberrations, bonding with their new figurine companions, and reliving memories they thought they'd left behind. But all of that was prologue. Transported back to Teldire, they arrived at the doorstep of [[Samwell]]'s cottage to find it had been broken into. Inside, past a porcelain doll begging for candy and a painting that served as a secret passage, the basement revealed a grim scene - bodies of plains marauders, blood-soaked hallways, and the remnants of whatever had gone wrong in [[Samwell]]'s absence.

But the basement held wonders too. [[Shamash]] found an arcane key hidden inside a hollowed-out book. [[Zalros]] identified a scrying portal and a Gem of Brightness. [[Brog]] made friends with two polymorphed ants who helped the party unlock hidden fossil displays, revealing dove maple, mushrooms, and fine carpenter's tools behind their doors. And [[Elara]] proved once again that a pocket full of birdseed can solve almost anything.

Then came the fight. A Helmed Horror - a massive, animated suit of armor radiating heat and wielding a jagged blade - ambushed the party in the corridor. [[Drakka]] and [[Zalros]] took nasty hits, but it was [[Drakka]] who caved in the construct's helmet with a decisive blow, turning it to dust. After a round of healing potions and fey mushrooms, the party pressed on to a final locked door. Inside, two new figurines sat on podiums - one charcoal, one green. But before anyone could celebrate, footsteps echoed from deeper in the tower. Something else was waiting. And that is where we pick up tonight!
