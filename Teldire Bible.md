# **CAMPAIGN INFO / PRE-SESSION 1 SNAPSHOT**
## Campaign Premise + hook! ✨ 

You are part of a Guild called the **Skeleton Crew**. Your purpose? To delve into **ruins or dungeons** that **Adventuring Guild** members have **perished** and **retrieve their corpses**. It's not a pleasant job, but with the way **the continent Teldire** is looking; **population dwindling** due to hard-to-cure **disease**, **the sacred giants** that protected the people are **slaughtered by dragons** and **those same dragons** are warring for territory-- we need as many **re-animated bodies** we can get to help keep **society afloat**! You may have not all worked with each other before or have barely spoken to each other, but you each have been chosen to be a **small party** for a **under-the-table job** by a dwarf man named **Vesamear Fig**. You'll be paid **200 gold pieces each** if you retrieve a specific body of a **blue dragonborn** from a recently discovered, what they are calling, "odd dug out". It seems like a usual job, I'm sure nothing will go wrong!

# **SESSION 3**

### What happened

- At the tower entrance, Miragryx **froze the party**; everyone’s minds **melded** and you experienced a **shared flashback montage** (each PC’s past).
    
- The flashbacks were “replayable” memories where each character could **change the outcome** of a key moment.
    

### Flashback montage (by character)

- Drakka: Sparring/training scene → learns they **can’t get time off** for their child’s birth → chooses to **run to the birth anyway** and shares the moment with their partner and newborn.
    
- Elara: Re-lives a **final night with her sister** (last time they spoke); she’s aware it’s the moment before they split paths (Elara to the frontier, sister staying behind).
    
- Brog: Finds a grassy mound that is actually a sleeping **hill giant** → originally would’ve left it asleep → chooses to **wake it** so it can **protect the forest** from an unnamed future threat.
    
- Shamash Fenkenkabradon: Protects a village from lions → discovers soldiers trying to **sell lion cubs** → stops them and **saves the cubs**.
    
- Zalros Mi’hen: Talks with Benadeer at an inn; Zalros convinces him to **stay longer**. Benadeer asks about Vesamear Fig and a recent experiment (a transfigured crow); the memory ends shortly after.
    

### Back in the present (Miragryx confrontation)

- Lacey, Orange, and Blue **attack Miragryx** and “power up” into stronger forms (Lacey → full displacer beast; Orange → wyrm; Blue → owlbear).
    
- Miragryx’s focus breaks; the party is **unfrozen** and able to act.
    
- After the party’s counterattack, Miragryx **vanishes into dust/mote** and leaves a threat along the lines of: _“You’ll regret making an enemy of me.”_ (setting her up as a recurring antagonist).
