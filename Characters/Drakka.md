## Personality

Drakka has sharp situational awareness - they're often the first to notice environmental oddities and threats. They tend toward decisive action; when they see something that needs doing, they do it without hesitation.

They have a playful side too - covering [[Brog]] with insects while he was casting a spell, throwing a spider specimen at [[Elara]] as a prank, and generally keeping the mood light when things aren't dire.

When things *are* dire, Drakka is a force. They dealt the decisive blow to the first Helmed Horror by caving in its helmet, and they took nasty hits alongside [[Zalros]] without backing down.
