## Personality

Shamash is friendly, hearty, and larger than life. He laughs too loud, talks too loud, and stands too close without noticing - and somehow that's part of his charm. He loves jokes, cards, drinking, and has a cigar habit. There's a gravelly warmth to him.

Despite the boisterous exterior, he can turn serious fast when duty demands it. He's a soldier through and through - experienced, dependable, and quietly protective of those around him.

He silently looks up to each of his party members and is content to step aside and let what he considers a crew of brilliant minds take charge, stepping in when his input is needed.

## Backstory

Gold was always the main motivator in Shamash's life. He's been an infantry soldier and mercenary for as long as anyone can remember, and he's lost count of how many missions he's taken in the name of GP. With no family or friends to worry about, he's had nothing to lose - though occasionally he does wonder what that kind of life would be like.

His real dream? A peaceful retirement. Somewhere secluded, in the middle of nowhere, a little paradise to call his own. Every copper earned is another step closer to that quiet life. He's confident, experienced, and treats every mission as routine - though he has to admit, this new team he's been assigned to does pique his interest.
