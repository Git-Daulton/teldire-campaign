## Personality

Elara is a pragmatic investigator with sharp instincts and a cool head. She's practical and clinical in the moment - the type to examine a body for materials without flinching, test a theory by shooting a wooden squirrel's head off, or chase down escaping ants and give them a firm stomp.

But beneath that pragmatism is a quiet respect for the dead. She stealthily places culturally significant tokens on the chests of fallen adventurers as their bodies are bagged - a private ritual she doesn't announce or draw attention to.

She has a dry wit and isn't easily shaken. When [[Drakka]] threw a spider specimen at her as a prank, she simply said they were kind of cute, and that her sister caught these all the time growing up.

What we know: Elara has a sister named **Asta Vale** who she is searching for. The two split paths at some point - Elara went to the frontier, while her sister stayed behind. The session 3 flashback revealed that Elara re-lived the final night she and her sister spoke, and she was acutely aware it would be the last time before they parted ways.

Elara also had an intense nightmare about her sister being deceased, which gave her 1 point of exhaustion. Whatever happened between them, finding Asta Vale clearly weighs heavily on her.

She also seems to relate to Miragryx's eagerness to return home - both share a desire to fix what's broken in the world they left behind.
