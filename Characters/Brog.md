## Personality

Brog is empathic, gentle, and deeply protective of small creatures. He's shy and nervous in conversation - lots of "uhm"s and "uh"s when he speaks - but when his friends or innocent creatures are in danger, a quiet courage rises to the surface.

He tries to communicate with any fauna he encounters, and gets genuinely upset when harmless beings are harmed. He's the kind of person who would bury a wooden squirrel out of respect, even knowing it wasn't truly alive.

Despite his gentle nature, Brog will turn fierce when it matters. He once blew the party's cover by standing up to confront the Death Kiss, driven by the desire to avenge his familiar Hootie.

## Abilities & Magic

### Homebrew Note
Brog's Wild Shape has been substituted/altered toward **summoning-based play** rather than traditional shapeshifting.

- **Detect Magic** - Used to identify wooden animals as Enchanted and Transmutation magic
