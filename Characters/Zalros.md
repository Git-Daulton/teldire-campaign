## Personality

Zalros is an endlessly cheerful soul who finds joy in nearly everything. He loves music (primarily the lute), poetry, and jokes. He can occasionally slip into a moment of melancholy when things turn serious, but he bounces back to his warm, positive self quickly.

He forms bonds with people easily and loves hearing their stories. He is the kind of person who, if you ask him about music or poetry, you've entered an unskippable cutscene of passionate rambling.

He writes frequently - notes, lyrics, impressions, thoughts. It's not a trauma response; he simply feels calmer when things are written down and secured rather than left to the volatility of memory.

---

## Backstory

Zalros and his brother **Benadeer** were traveling scholars who spent many years crossing Teldire, combing through musty libraries, meeting wizened hermits, and studying the arcane on an impeccable level.

During an adventure through the southern realms, Benadeer caught a foul illness that had been plaguing a neighbouring town. No apothecary, priest, or healer would risk treating him. Desperate, Zalros inscribed the ground with forbidden runes and spoke words he never imagined he'd say aloud. Something answered.

Zalros offered the totality of his arcane knowledge in exchange for the power to channel healing energy into his brother. The bargain was accepted - his mind was purged of everything he'd spent his life learning, and in return, his bones warmed like embers and his skin tingled with newfound power.

It wasn't enough. Benadeer had already succumbed to his illness before the deal was even complete.

Now Zalros carries journals and tomes he can no longer understand, seeks to retrace a history he can barely remember, and quietly wonders what exactly he got himself into when he made a pact with something he can hardly hope to fathom.

## Memory Rules

These were explicitly established between Daulton and the DM:

- **Arcane research / discoveries** - Completely gone.
- **In-between memories** - Spotty. Blurry where important discussions overlapped with everyday moments.
- **Everyday / social memories** - Mostly intact. He remembers nights at inns, foods, routes, potholes on roads, people he met.
- **Historical / religious knowledge** - Intact, unless tied to arcane specifics. He might remember the layout of a mage college, but not which classroom taught Abjuration or where the Transmutation library was.

## Earlier History (Blurry)

> [!warning] Spoiler-Sensitive
> This section contains backstory details discussed between Daulton and the DM. Some of this is deliberately unclear to Zalros himself.

- Zalros apprenticed under **Dr. Zilheir** - a tall, slender, broad-shouldered human with quaffed dark hair greying at the sides, circular glasses, and a face that looked partially patched together.
- A fellow pupil was **Saulmin** - a dwarf of similar age to Zalros, with slicked-back light brown hair, glasses, and green eyes with an earthy rim. They met as early teens.
- Zalros' memories of studying under Dr. Zilheir are almost entirely blurry - a dim lab, a chalkboard, writing, then blackouts. He can't hold water to what the relationship was really like.
- What he remembers most are stolen glances from Saulmin, a beach visit with Saulmin and Benadeer, water balloons, laughter.
- This period ended abruptly with an argument between Zalros' father and Dr. Zilheir. His father declared that Zalros would not see Zilheir anymore. In the memory, Zilheir didn't look the same - taller, bigger, his face different. Zalros chalks this up to memories blurring together.
- After that, Benadeer taught Zalros everything else he needed. This is what kicked off their years as traveling brothers.

## The Shamash "Muscle Memory" Hook

When [[Shamash]] casts wizard-adjacent Eldritch Knight spells, Zalros gets goosebumps and an involuntary tension in his hands and throat - as if his body anticipates the somatic and verbal components before they happen. It's not sadness, not regained memory, and not training. It's a strange physical recognition.

During downtime, Zalros asks casual curiosity questions:
- *"How did you move your hands for Chill Touch again? Like... this?"*
- *"How did you curl your tongue on that last syllable?"*

This is pure RP texture with no mechanical effect.
