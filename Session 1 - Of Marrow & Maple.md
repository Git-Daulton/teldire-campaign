The party found themselves transported back to Teldire, arriving in front of Samwell's old abode. In front of them stood a modest, aged cottage with an attached two-story tower. Taking but a moment to enjoy the familiar air and golden-wheat field scenery, Zalros began by making his way to the front of the door. The door appeared already opened and very slightly ajar, with evidence of force being used to open the door.

Peeking his head in slightly, Zalros immediately toke note of a few shapes in the dark cabin:
	-Chair with a rope potentially indicating a hostage-like situation
	-What looks to be a carpet covered in gems potentially? It's hard to make out

Elara decided to join Zalros in stepping inside, causing sensor lights to activate and illuminate the space. With the new light, they are able to see shattered glass and two silver vases with red marbles inlaid that are tipped on their side. They also take note of a few other objects in the room:
	-Candlestick with blue marbles engraved into it
	-Silver water pitcher / vase with a blue marble
	-Porcelain Doll

Against the far wall were three paintings. On the left, a photo of the coast. The right, a photo of a back owlbear, which looks quite similar to Oswald. The middle photo, vertical and the largest of the bunch, is of a young Firbolg assumed to be Samuel. He is with a Hippogryph in the photo. 

Brog began to approach the doll, but Elara was dedicated to test the painting, remembering that Samuel advised the party to enter his basement from a "painting from his youth". Brog began panicking as he began to hear voices coming from the doll, but Elara had successfully began to enter the painting, so the party began to move together as one into the room behind the painting. Parting with the doll, Brog heard it say "I only wanted candy...".

Arriving at the bottom of the stairs, the party discovered a small hallway with a door at the end. Elara went to check the door, discovering it had an arcane lock which had been unsuccessfully picked. The first notch of the arcane lock had failed while being picked, causing a brief fiery burst to destroy the lock. The lock being destroyed, the party continued into the next room.

In the next room the party found an interesting room containing a few bookshelves and display cases containing insect specimens and other oddities. Zalros begins looking through a bookshelf, and finds a book which gives him advantage against aberrations. Brog locates and takes some books on creature / monster biology. Shamash finds a book in the bookcase which is hollowed out and contains a small, arcane key. 

Taking note of some displays, the party notices a fossil with some missing pieces in the room. Brog confirms that there seems to be some connection between the fossil and the bookcase, as they share the same framing / border.

Elara decides to go back and check a potential other path in the first hallway after the stairs; checking closely, she finds a sliding door and opens it to find a room containing a cabinet with a geode and a terracotta vase, with a chest next to that. On the other wall, there's a cabinet with a bag containing bird seed with other geodes and rocks on it. Elara looks into some of the displays and finds one goldfish that looks very unlike the others; it has the head of a dragon, and a single silver scale on it. Elara also finds a fossil piece hidden within a structure of a small bird. 

Investigating the geodes further, Elara found an amethyst geode that contains some sort of magical property worth about 15gp. Elara takes a pocket full of birdseed, and heads back towards the group. Drakka attempts to prank Elara by throwing a spider specimen at her, but Elara responds by saying they're kind of cute, and that her sister caught these all the time growing up.

Elara tells the party of what she saw, and the party returns to the other room to investigate the chest. Drakka covers Brog with insects before they leave, as Brog is casting a ritual spell to summon a familiar. In the other room, Shamash uses the key he found to open the chest, and discovers a glowing pearl-like orb. Zalros casts Identify, and discovers the following information about the object:

> Gem of Brightness

Brog completes summoning his two familiars - a bat-like creature and a pseudodragon. He asks them to scout ahead, and the party continues down the next hallway ahead with Zalros in the lead. Zalros smells an "iron-like" smells, and sees what appears to be blood on the ground. Investigating further, it appears to be quite a massacare, with blood continuing down the stairs ahead. Brog sends his familiars to investigate, and they confirm there is a body on the floor below. It has been absolutely brutalized into smithereens and is barely recognizable. 

The party continues down into the hallway, which has a flickering light. Zalros takes the lead into a room which contains an autopsy table made of wood, and a pillar with a bowl of black, swirling material inside. The party investigates the corpse further, and Elara checks it for any materials. Finding some gold, silver, and some stolen wizard components, Zalros removes the blood with prestidigitation and the party notes the body appears to be a plains marauder, similar to the ones they first encountered in the dugout. 

Zalros begins casting identify on the bowl as the party continues on. Drakka leaves the party and investigates another room containing a book case as well as many knickknacks and specimens. Drakka finds another fossil missing pieces in this room, and Elara puts the fossil she found earlier inside. The bookcase shudders and glows a bit.

The party continues to another room containing a body and a large glass aquarium. Elara checks the body, finding a silver dagger and some more money. Brog discovers a large entity inside the aquarium, and Shamash uses the stone to commune with Samuel and ask about the bodies and the entity in the aquarium. Samuel replies that the entity is Jerry, and doesn't know about the bodies. Brog confirms that Jerry is a "seahorse" based on his newly obtained books, and discovers that Jerry may be up to 3,000 years old.

Investigating the room further, Elara finds a fin piece and places it back in the other fossil, causing the same shudder / glow to occur. Zalros completes his Identification of the bowl and discovers it is a scrying portal with the following information:

> - **Creatures:** You can target any creature on the same plane of existence, provided you know them or have a physical connection (like a lock of hair or photo). The sensor moves with the creature, allowing you to follow their movements, conversations, and immediate surroundings.
> - **Locations:** You can target a specific location that you have seen before. The sensor remains fixed in that location and does not move. This is ideal for scouting, such as viewing a door, a specific room, or an area of a dungeon.
> - **No Plane Hopping:** _Scrying_ cannot see onto other planes of existence; the target must be on the same plane as the caster

The party continues on down the hallway further, noting a melted sword and further viscera trailing away from them. They discover a pile of viscera, a body beheaded and the floor covered in blood. Off to the side, they note another hallway, with a podium containing another bowl like the scrying portal. The water is different - it's more clear yet foggy. Behind that is a large ant farm with two ants who appear to stand out from the rest.

Brog communicates with them and discovers they are two polymorphed humans that have been in there living as ants for almost 15 years. The party agreed to take them along in return for helping them obtain the fossil pieces that were within the ant colony. Using the newly obtained fossil pieces, the party splits and places the remaining pieces within the fossil displays they found earlier.

Behind the fish fossil, Elara, Zalros, and Brog find some bundles of dove maple, as well as fine carpenter's tools that they take along. The dove maple is stamped with the name Alqua - a keeper of woods in the winter court. Behind the rat fossil, Drakka and Shamash find a small living biome of moss, vines, fireflies and mushrooms. 

During their exploration, Brog's familiars return to him terrifying, noting that something is coming quickly and they'd best prepare. The team behind the fish door begins to return to find Shamash and Drakka fighting a massive suit of living armor, heat radiating from it's red essence binding it together. It wielded a massive, jagged blade. 

The party had an intense fight, with Drakka and Zalros taking some nasty hits from the large blade. The animated suit of armor attacked relentlessly, both swinging and attempting to grab party members; it attacked with the finesse of a well-trained soldier. Drakka dealt a decisive blow to it's helmet, caving it in and causing the creation to fade into dust. 

Once safe, the party took a moment to consume some healing potions and take a moment to grasp the situation. Drakka consumed some of the mushrooms they found, and noted that they got a pleasant feeling that gave them advantage against deception and persuasion from fey. The rest of the party consumed some as well, to varying lengths of effect. Brog consumed one too many, and began to feel a bit queasy, seeing a door to another dimension shimmer in and out of existence briefly.

After giving the ant-people a pinch of birdseed, the party continued to another room which contained a grand arcane lock. This matched the key that Zalros had from Samuel; opening the door, the party found two podiums with cushions. One held a small wooden figure painted the colour of charcoal, the other held a figure painted green. 

Before the session ended, Brog had heard some clanking and footsteps from a hallway further ahead. The party had began to think a bit about the encounter; it was likely the broken vases on the first floor which had summoned these creations. With two vases broken, it's safe to assume there is likely another one waiting for the party ahead! With the session ended, we'll have to wait until next time to find out!
